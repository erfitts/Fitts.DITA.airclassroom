Welcome to Airclassroom!

Founded in November of 2016 and based in Blacksburg, Virginia, Airclassroom is a trusted community marketplace for people to list, discover, and book unique accommodations around Montgomery County, online or from an iPhone or iPad.

Whether it is a classroom for a study session or a classroom for a business proposal and presentation, Airclassroom connects people to unique locations in and around Virginia Tech's campus, at any price point, in Southwest, Virginia. And with world-class customer service and a growing community of users, Airclassroom is the easiest way for faculty and staff to monetize their extra space and showcase it to an audience of thousands.
Note: The description is adapted from Airbnb's "About Us" web page.
